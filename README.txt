JACK CRANN - AUTHOR WEBSITE
============================

Modern Awwwards-style author website for Jack Crann,
author of "The Affection Connection" and founder of Peace of Mind Canine.

FOLDER STRUCTURE
----------------
Jack-Crann-Author-Website/
├── index.html          (Home Page)
├── about.html          (About Jack)
├── book.html           (The Book)
├── services.html       (Services & Booking)
├── faq.html            (FAQ)
├── contact.html        (Contact)
├── css/
│   ├── custom.min.css  (Main Styles)
│   └── responsive.css  (Responsive Styles)
├── js/
│   └── main.js         (Main JavaScript)
└── images/             (Image folder - add your images here)
    ├── jack-crann.png
    ├── book-cover.jpeg
    └── book-back-cover.jpeg

SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
------------------
1. Extract the ZIP folder
2. Add your images to the /images/ folder:
   - jack-crann.png       (Jack's professional photo)
   - book-cover.jpeg       (Front cover of The Affection Connection)
   - book-back-cover.jpeg  (Back cover of The Affection Connection)
3. Open index.html in any modern browser
