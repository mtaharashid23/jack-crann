About The Author

Meet Jack Crann

Growing up on the Connecticut shore, Jack was always the kid dogs followed home. What started as childhood curiosity evolved into a lifelong passion and a 30+ year career dedicated to understanding and teaching the art of human-canine communication.

After a 20-year professional dance career, Jack attended National K9 School for Dog Trainers in Columbus, Ohio, where he was certified as a master trainer. He then founded Peace of Mind Canine, offering personalized training for you and your dog.

"Do what you love and love what you do. I have been blessed to do just that for over 3 decades."
