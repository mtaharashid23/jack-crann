About The Book

A Refreshing Approach to Dog Training

For more than three decades, Jack Crann has helped thousands of dogs and their owners build stronger, happier relationships through understanding, patience, and trust.

In The Affection Connection, Jack shares unforgettable true stories, practical training insights, and life lessons gathered from a lifetime devoted to teaching both people and dogs. From overcoming common behavioral challenges to preventing problems before they begin, this book offers a refreshing approach that focuses on education rather than control.
